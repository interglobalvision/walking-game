How to play

so you don't know what you're doing is that it? this is silly. i mean why are you even playing this game at all?.....anyways i gueeeeess i'll explain how this works. *eyeroll* .....

so its pretty simple. the game is called "walking game" which means...drumroll... you have to walk to play. your goal here is to be the best walker in the whole world, and youve gotta train to get there. look, now theres a circle with an arrow in it at the bottom-left of your screen there...see that? thats your compass. so you need to look at that and follow whichever way the arrow points. if you run into an obstacle like a ...tree...or a....building...walk around that, and keep heading toward where the arrow points you. try not to get hit by a car or trespass... because if that happens? its on you pal! anyways...as you walk, a golden flag will appear on screen - thats your goal. as you continue, you'll see the golden goal flag getting closer. and when you reach that flag? time for a minigame challenge. the minigame challenge wins you points. and what do points win you? respect. not from me of course. but you can share your progress on feisbuuk or twinder or whatever and showoff to your "friend".

does that clear things up? if not, you should probably just play another game like... idk... jumpy fish or matchy marbles...whatever
